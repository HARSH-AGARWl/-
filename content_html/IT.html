It chapter 1 :-

         

               


        IT Chapter 1 is a horror movie based on the novel by Stephen King. The film follows a group of seven children in the small town of Derry, Maine, who are terrorized by a shape-shifting entity that primarily takes the form of a clown named Pennywise.

        The story begins in 1988 when Georgie, the younger brother of one of the main characters, goes missing after encountering Pennywise. As the other children begin to have their own terrifying encounters with the creature, they band together to try and defeat it.
        
        The children soon realize that Pennywise is not just a simple clown but is in fact an ancient entity that feeds on fear and has been terrorizing the town of Derry for centuries. As the group faces their own fears and personal struggles, they must work together to try and defeat Pennywise once and for all.
        
        As the film builds to its climactic conclusion, the children are forced to confront their deepest fears and find the courage to stand up to Pennywise, leading to a thrilling and terrifying final showdown.
