Tu Jhoothi Main Makkaar :-

         

               


        "Tu Jhoothi Main Makkaar" is a Hindi phrase that can be translated to "You're a liar, and I'm a deceiver." It is not the title of a specific work but rather a common phrase used to express a situation where both parties are being dishonest with each other.

        The phrase may be used in various contexts, such as in a romantic relationship where one partner is lying and the other is pretending not to know, or in a business deal where both parties are being deceitful to gain an advantage.
        
        In popular culture, "Tu Jhoothi Main Makkaar" has been used as a song title in several Bollywood movies, including "Ek Ladki Ko Dekha Toh Aisa Laga" (2019) and "Kala Bazaar" (1989).
